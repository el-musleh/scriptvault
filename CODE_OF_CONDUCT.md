# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are dedicated to providing an environment that fosters collaboration, growth, and mutual respect. To ensure that our community remains inclusive and welcoming, we have adopted the Contributor Covenant as the code of conduct for this project.

## Our Standards

- **Respectful Communication**: Use courteous language. Avoid harassment or demeaning commentary.
- **Accountability**: Report bugs quickly, maintain transparency, and follow up on actions.
- **Inclusive Behavior**: Welcome contributors from all backgrounds, skill levels, and identities.
- **Open‑Ended Collaboration**: Encourage idea exchange; no fear of rejection.

## Enforcement

Violations will be handled separately, but may result in temporary or permanent bans from the repository, community, or public channels.
